Medical Facilities

  • An airy, well ventilated room with beds and basic medical facilities define the health care centre of Joshitha Hyderabad.
  • The informative charts and posters on the walls describing the importance of a healthy body welcome the little patients.
  • The Infirmary can handle situations by providing immediate relief in the form of first-aid.
  • An mini van is also present in the school to take students to the local hospital in case of an emergency.
