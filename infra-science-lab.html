Science Lab

Science is essentially a practical subject which emphasizes on experimental work and learning by doing. Experiments carried in our school laboratories are an integral part of the teaching and learning process of the subject. The students are encouraged to test theories prove concepts and validate hypotheses. It helps students to remember the concepts better.
The school has separate labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Home Science. The labs are well equipped with the latest equipments, specimens, chemicals / reagents and appliances. It is here that the skills, aptitudes and abilities are sharpened and the students know how to apply practically the knowledge they have gained.

PHYSICS LAB

The Physics lab allows for a complete practical experience in kinematics to electricity.

CHEMISTRY LAB

From titrations to deducing boiling points, our Chemistry lab is the perfect place for every experiment. We have a range of chemicals used for a variety of experiments and practical work.

BIOLOGY LAB

The Biology lab allows students to examine and investigate the wonders of life. With microscopes and specimens (both plant and animal), students can explore, experiment and discover the wonders of nature beyond the classroom.

SAFETY IS OUR PRIORITY

All students wear lab coats, goggles and gloves when handling chemicals, furthermore, no experiments are conducted without the supervision of a professional. In addition, safety posters are displayed in all labs to ensure an educational and safe experience when dealing with equipment and chemicals.
