Aims and Objective

  • Provide the kind of environment best suited to the social development of the individual.
  • Develop basic mental abilities and skills and to help in the physical growth and proficiency in games.
  • Enable students to develop the qualities of leadership, self-confidence and self respect.
  • To help learn how to establish good human relationships, imbibe virtues of moral courage, social discipline and to acquire economic understanding.
  • Develop the all round personality and bring out the best of the student.
  • Conversation in English only, so as to develop confidence and communication skills.
  • To encourage the students to take responsibility for their own actions and behaviour.
  • To help us to achieve our goals, parents are welcome to meet the principal, and the teachers on a regular basis to discuss problems relating to academics and offer their valuable suggestions.
