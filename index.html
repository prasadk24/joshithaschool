What We Do

Joshitha School is setup in calm, safe and secured environment. Joshitha School is located in a prime area, but yet bit away from traffic to ensure children are away from pollution due to vehicles. This center has ample of open space both outdoor and indoor for daily exercise and development of motor skills for childern.
