Admission Process

Stage 1
  • Visit the School
  • Fill the enquiry form.
  • Take a tour of the school
  • Meet with Academic team to discuss about Curriculum and Teaching Methodologies.
  • Collect the Fee structure from Councilor.
Stage 2
  • Academic Aptitude Test: The written test comprises English, Mathematics and EVS/Science
  • Interview with student
  • Interaction with Parents
Stage 3
  • Collect the Admission application form, from the Councilor.
  • Submit the Admission form with necessary documents.
  • Six passport size photographs and two stamp size photographs of the student. Parents One Each Photograph.
  • Certified copy of School Report of the previous academic year
  • One certified copy of Child's Birth Certificate.
  • Copy of the T.C. (If possible).
